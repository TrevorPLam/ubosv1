Proposal Builder
Drag-and-Drop Editor: Visual document builder with layout containers and content blocks. Drag Structures (rows, columns, sections) and Blocks (text, images, video, tables, signatures, forms) onto the canvas. Two-tab interface: Content (build document) and Design (global styling, fonts, colors). Real-time preview as you build.

Interactive Pricing Tables: Dynamic pricing blocks where prospects can select quantities, toggle optional add-ons, choose between packages, and see totals update in real time. Supports multi-tier pricing, volume discounts, and recurring billing line items. Calculations are automated to prevent manual errors.

Content Library: Central repository of saved content blocks—case studies, team bios, service descriptions, testimonials, terms and conditions—that can be pulled into any proposal. Eliminates rewriting the same content across documents. Sections stay consistent across all proposals when updated centrally.

Client Input Forms: Embeddable form fields within proposals where prospects submit information (project requirements, company details, preferences) directly inside the document before signing.

Rich Media Embedding: Insert images (uploaded directly), videos (embedded from YouTube, Vidyard, Wistia, or uploaded), interactive charts, and maps into proposals. Transforms static PDFs into engaging, interactive web experiences.

AI Proposal Generation: Generate complete, formatted proposals from simple prompts. Input a website URL plus a description of the prospect and offering; AI creates a fully structured proposal with company-specific information, professional formatting, and relevant sections—cutting creation time from hours to minutes.

AI Content Assistant: Writing assistance within the editor for section copy, executive summaries, scope descriptions, and value propositions. AI-generated content stays within brand voice guidelines and approved phrasing.

AI Pricing Table Generator: Automatically builds persuasive pricing tables with tiered options based on service type, target sector, and pricing strategy. Creates upgrade paths designed to increase conversions and deal size.

Templates
Template Library: 100+ pre-built, customizable templates organized by industry (consulting, construction, creative services, SaaS, legal) and use case (project proposal, service agreement, retainer, sponsorship, partnership). Templates contain pre-written section outlines, placeholder content, and recommended structures for specific business scenarios.

Custom Template Builder: Create organization-specific templates from scratch or by modifying existing ones. Lock brand elements (logos, colors, fonts), section structure, terms, and pricing logic so every proposal across the team stays consistent.

Template Variables & Merge Fields: Insert dynamic placeholders for contact name, company name, deal value, project dates, and custom CRM fields. When a proposal is generated from a deal, all fields auto-populate—eliminating manual entry and copy-paste errors.

Brand Customization: Apply brand colors, logos, fonts, and imagery globally across all templates. Custom domain mapping so prospects visit proposals.yourdomain.com without third-party branding in the URL. White-label presentation throughout.

Document Management & Workflow
Proposal Creation from Pipeline: Generate proposals directly from CRM deals. Contact details, deal value, product interest, and conversation history auto-fill from the deal record. Proposal status syncs bidirectionally—when a proposal is viewed, signed, or rejected, the deal stage updates automatically.

Approval Workflows: Define conditions that trigger internal approval: deal size above threshold, discount percentage exceeds limit, non-standard terms included. Approved or rejected through the same attention queue used across the platform. Configurable rules keep deals moving without losing oversight.

Version Control: Every edit, revision, and version change is logged with timestamps and author attribution. Proposals lock permanently upon signature to create an immutable record. Side-by-side version comparison for audits.

Proposal Status Tracking: Visual status workflow—Draft → In Review → Sent → Opened → Viewed → Signed → Accepted → Rejected → Expired. Status visible on pipeline cards and in proposal list view.

Auto-Reminders: Automated follow-up emails to prospects who haven't opened or signed their proposal. Configurable intervals and messaging. AI agents can customize reminder content based on prospect engagement history.

PDF Export: Generate a pixel-perfect PDF version matching the interactive web version for offline sharing, printing, or compliance archiving.

E-Signature
Embedded Signing: Signature fields placed directly within the proposal—no separate application or account required. Signers receive a magic link, open the proposal, and sign in-browser without downloading or creating an account.

Signature Field Configuration: Place signature fields, initial fields, and date fields anywhere in the document. Configure signing order for multi-party signatures. Set counter-signer requirements where internal team members sign after the client.

Signing Progress Tracking: Real-time visibility into which signers have completed their portion and which are pending. Automated reminders to incomplete signers.

Legally Binding: Signatures captured with IP address, timestamp, and device fingerprint for legal validity. Compliance with e-signature regulations including ESIGN, UETA, and eIDAS.

Signer Authentication: Magic link authentication via email. Optional SMS-based verification or knowledge-based authentication for higher-risk agreements.

Engagement Tracking & Analytics
Real-Time View Tracking: Instant notifications when a prospect opens the proposal. Track every subsequent view, total time spent, and last-viewed timestamp.

Page-by-Page Analytics: Detailed breakdown showing exactly which pages and sections the prospect spent time on, how long they spent on pricing, and which sections they skipped. Identifies where interest is highest and where objections may be forming.

Engagement Scoring: Composite score per proposal based on opens, time spent, pages viewed, and sharing behavior. High engagement scores signal buying intent and prioritize follow-up activity.

Sharing Detection: Detect when a proposal is forwarded to additional stakeholders not on the original send list. Surfaces new decision-makers and influencers for the sales team to engage.

Proposal Analytics Dashboard: Aggregate reporting across all proposals: view rates, time-to-open, time-to-sign, close rates by template and sales rep, average engagement duration, and rejection reasons. Trend analysis over time.

Optimized Follow-Ups: AI-driven recommendations for when and how to follow up based on prospect engagement patterns. Trigger alerts when a prospect shows high intent behavior—repeated pricing page views, long reading sessions, or quick re-opening.
